Help & FAQs

Quick answers
  • Signing in: Make sure you’re on the latest version of gdgt+. If you forgot your password, use Forgot Password from the sign-in screen.
  • Searching manuals: Try exact product names or model numbers. If no results, please send the product link and we’ll add it.
  • App issues: From the app, go to Profile → Support → Feedback Form to attach logs.

Account deletion

You can initiate account deletion in the gdgt+ app at Profile → Delete my account → Confirm. If a browser step is needed, we’ll send you to the exact page to finish deletion and email you a confirmation when it’s complete.

Data deletion removes personal information and associated content except where retention is legally required (e.g., billing/anti-fraud). Learn more in our Privacy Policy and Terms.
